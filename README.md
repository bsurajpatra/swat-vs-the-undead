# Swat vs the undead

A high-octane 3D First-Person Shooter game built in Unity where you must survive waves of the undead in a post-apocalyptic arena.

"The world has fallen. The dead have risen. This arena is your final stand."

In this FPS survival game, you are dropped into a hostile environment with no backup. Your objective is simple: Kill or be killed. You must manage your ammunition and health while fighting off relentless waves of zombies. As you progress through the levels, the challenge intensifies with more enemies and tighter time limits.

## âœ¨ Features

- **Level-Based Progression**: Test your skills across 3 levels of increasing difficulty:
  - **Level 1**: 20 Zombies | 3 Minutes
  - **Level 2**: 30 Zombies | 4 Minutes
  - **Level 3**: 40 Zombies | 5 Minutes
- **Dynamic Enemy AI**: Zombies will patrol, detect, chase, and attack you. Listen for their growls as they get closer!
- **Combat System**: satisfying gunplay with realistic shooting, reloading, and muzzle flash effects.
- **Resource Management**: Scavenge the arena for **Ammo Boxes** and **Medicine Kits** to stay in the fight.
- **Immersive Environment**: Features dynamic audio, helicopter extraction visuals, and story-driven intro.
- **Modern Input Support**: Built using Unity's new Input System for smooth and responsive controls.
